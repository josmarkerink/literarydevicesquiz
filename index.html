Try to experiment with these devices in your  own writing and see what you discover
A metaphor is when an object, thing or person is described as something else for descriptive purposes
An oxymoron is where two contradictory terms appear together
Alliteration is where you use the same sound at the beginning of a string of words
A simile is the comparison of one thing to another
A rhetorical question is a question that is not meant to be answered
Personification is where objects or non-living things are given human-like characteristics
